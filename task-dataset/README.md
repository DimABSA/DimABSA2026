## Track A: DimABSA datasets
- 3 subtasks: DimASR, DimASTE, DimASQP
- 4 domains: Restaurant, Laptop, Hotel and Finance
- 6 Languages: Chinese, English, Japanese, Russian, Tatar, and Ukrainian
- A total of 76,958 aspect instances (aspect pairs, triplets, and quadruplets) across 42,590 sentences.
- Categorical versions are also provided by converting VA scores into polarity labels (positive, neutral, and negative).
## Track B DimStance datasets
- 1 subtask: DimASR
- 2 domains: environmental protection and politics
- 5 Languages: Chinese, English, German, Nigerian Pidgin, and Swahili
- A total of 11,746 stance targets across 7,365 texts
